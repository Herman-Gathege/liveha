Who is LIVEHA?

LIVEHA is a singer, songwriter, producer, and sound engineer born and bred in Nairobi. His soulful sound brings raw emotion and authenticity you can feel it in every note and hear it in every track. Simply put, LIVEHA is a breath of fresh air in today’s music landscape.
He is proudly signed under SONSOFZION INC.

My music is a mirror of the soul vulnerable, fierce, spiritual.
I don’t just make tracks, I tell stories.
Each beat, each word, comes from a place of truth.
This is more than art. It’s my calling.

Featured Tracks
