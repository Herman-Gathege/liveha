The Rise of LIVEHA: East African Artist Turning Heads

The dynamic East African duo LIVEHA and GARVEY are making waves in the music scene with their genre-blending sound and bold creative vision. Known for their electric performances and soulful storytelling, the pair recently debuted a visual and sound experience that stunned audiences both online and in person.

Their latest project, shot in Nairobi and infused with elements of Afrofuturism, urban grit, and cultural pride, has sparked conversations about the future of East African music and identity.

With roots deeply embedded in their community and eyes set on the global stage, LIVEHA is proving that independent artists can lead with authenticity, excellence, and power.
