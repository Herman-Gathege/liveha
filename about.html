LIVEHA

Producer. Sound Engineer. Songwriter. Visionary.
Independent artist pushing sonic boundaries.

My music is a mirror of the soul vulnerable, fierce, spiritual.

The Journey

From Nairobi’s underground creative scene to digital platforms worldwide, my journey has been one of growth, grit, and transformation. Influenced by soulful Afrobeats, gospel harmonies, and gritty hip hop, my sound is ever-evolving but always true.
